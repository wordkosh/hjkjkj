Identity theft

What is identity theft? and its Defination-


Identity theft occurs when someone uses another person's personal identifying information, like their name, identifying number, or credit card number, without their permission, to commit fraud or other crimes.


In simple words, a hacker clones your profile and do crimes with name of you.

For eg. A girl name is Namrita and her friends name is sunita, a hacker has cloned Namrita facebook profile and started talking to Sunita and asked her for some money and she gave some money to the hacker thinking of Namrita, this is called Identity theft.


When someone use your profile to do cyber crimes, without your knowledge is known as Identity theft.
